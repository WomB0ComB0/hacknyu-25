Overview

Genie Who?

We’re a lightweight browser extension designed to give you personalized spending insights, making it easier than ever to shop smarter and save more.

Our Mission?

To help you make the most of your money—whether that means cutting back on unnecessary spending or finding ways to stretch your budget further.

Banks Thrive

Banks thrive on more transactions, and our purpose aligns perfectly with theirs: while they encourage shopping, we help you save along the way. With Genie, you can shop online, save more, and enjoy the best of both worlds.

Features

We created this extension to help you better understand your finances and provide an easy way to spend wisely and save more.

Spending Alerts

Display pop-ups when spending patterns exceed predefined thresholds.

Tracks Online Purchases

Monitors your online purchases using your transaction history.

Insightful Suggestions

Genie analyzes your spending habits, offering insights into recurring patterns and their costs.
